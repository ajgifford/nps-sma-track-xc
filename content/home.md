# Welcome to Nativity & St. Michael's Running Programs

## About Our Programs

Welcome to the home of the **Nativity** and **St. Michael's** running programs! We are dedicated to developing young athletes through Cross Country and Track & Field.

### Our Mission

To provide a positive, supportive environment where student-athletes can:
- Develop their running abilities
- Build character and discipline
- Foster teamwork and sportsmanship
- Achieve personal and team goals

### Program Highlights

**Cross Country (Fall Season)**
- Competitive meets throughout the season
- Team building activities
- Personal best tracking
- Championship opportunities

**Track & Field (Spring Season)**
- Multiple event specialties
- Individual and relay competitions
- Skills development
- Championship meets

### Get Involved

Whether you're a returning athlete or new to running, we welcome you to join our programs. Check out our schedules, meet our coaches, and explore our resources to get started!
